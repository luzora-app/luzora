Hi,

Last year, I spoke with a few people about the stress of keeping up with airdrops, testnets, claim pages, dashboards, deadlines, and all the small tasks that can easily get lost.

A lot of the feedback was the same: people were relying on memory, sticky notes, open tabs, community posts, and random reminders. Some had missed deadlines. Some had too many projects to track properly. Some just wanted a simpler way to come back to the right page at the right time.

That feedback helped shape what became Luzora.

Luzora is a browser extension that lets you turn any webpage into a task reminder. If there is a page you need to come back to, whether it is an airdrop dashboard, a claim page, a job application, a course, a research tab, or a tool you use often, Luzora helps you save it, set what needs to be done, and return to it when it matters.

We are now opening a small private test, and I wanted to send you a special invite because you were part of the early conversations that helped validate the problem.

Your feedback would mean a lot at this stage. We are not just looking for people to click around. We are looking for people who understand the pain of losing track of important web tasks and can help us build something useful.
